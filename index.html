What's Under The Hood

Under The Hood is a PC building workshop which aims at enriching the often neglected aspects of Computer Science. In an endeavor to promote hardware computer science, we intend to stage an event which is a first of its kind in VIT.

A student driven organisation to promote hardware oriented aspects of technology in a unique way. We are a bunch of students trying to change the way people look at Computer Science in general. To that end, we organize workshops, hands-on sessions & casual, interactive knowledge sharing sessions. We also extend our services to clients & corporate setups. An offshoot of the Computer Society of India VITU, Under The Hood is an initiative to promote, put to use Computer Science & technology in a unique way. In an endeavor to promote hardware computer science, we intend to stage an event which will be the first of its kind in VIT, at graVITas'14.

Complete noob or a hardware enthusiast - there's a rig for everyone. We teach you how to build it.

We are the COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA

DESIGNERS, DEVELOPERS, THINKERS and COMPUTER SCIENCE ENTHUSIASTS, working togther on building solutions, services, products no matter how trivial that FIT right in your routine and make a difference. This is us, Computer Society of India - VIT University.
We are a multi-disciplinary University Chapter with a sole aim to hone technology and push it forward. Projects, research, competitive programming, and a lot more to add on to our swag.

The mission of our Student Chapter in VIT University is to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the industry and helping them to integrate into the IT community.

We've organized Under The Hood, Riddler, Yuvana, Web Craft, Laser Tag and many such events and workshops. Mostly, we like to have a lot of fun.

We don't play softball. When we build, it matters.
